  • BSc Data Science

    The BSc Data Science aims to meet the current demand for graduates with data analysis skills in the commercial sector. This BSc provides a strong foundation in mathematics, statistics, and computer science and the skills and knowledge to tackle real-world problems. You will learn cutting-edge techniques for collecting, storing, analysing and visualising data and extracting information to inform decision-making across various organisations, which are essential for a career as a data scientist or data-driven software developer.
