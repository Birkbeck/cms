School of Computing and Mathematical Sciences

What is this?

  • A series of free course to ”kick-off” your studies
  • Eases you (back) into studying, whether that be part-time or full-time
  • Covers (roughly) the first two weeks of your chosen programme of study
  • Six to Ten sessions, in the evening, 6 pm to 8 pm.
  • Online and in-person (HyFlex)

Three versions

  • Computer Science — September
  • Data Science — September, December
  • Mathematics — September
